Brand name is Carethia (renamed from Careverse). All references must use Carethia.

## Carethia Design System

- **Primary**: Coral/rose `355 70% 60%`
- **Secondary**: Teal `174 42% 45%`
- **Accent**: Warm gold `35 90% 62%`
- **Background**: Warm cream `30 50% 98%`
- **Fonts**: Playfair Display (display), DM Sans (body)
- **Buttons**: Rounded-full with `hero` and `hero-outline` variants
- **Trust colors**: Green `145 60% 45%`, Gold `42 90% 55%`
- **Cards**: `.service-card` class, `shadow-card` / `shadow-card-hover`
- **Target market**: Bangkok, Thailand (THB pricing)

## Pages
- `/` - Landing page (hero, services, how-it-works, trust, featured caregivers, CTA, footer)
- `/browse` - Browse caregivers with filters
- `/auth` - Login/Signup with Member/Caregiver role selection
- `/dashboard` - Role-based dashboard (member bookings vs caregiver revenue/requests)
